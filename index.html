Impossible Color Viewer

Background

Extraspatial colors are visible by crossing your eyes on conflicting colors (e.g. red and green) to see a color which is not just a mix (like brown) but has the properties of both (an impossible red-green).

Chimerical colors are created by tricking your eyes into percieving impossible colors such as black with impossible saturation (Stygian), brighter than white (Self-Luminous), or more than 100% saturated (Hyperbolic).

Instructions

Extraspatial - Click on a preset, or enter hex codes for two contrasting colors or use a preset. Cross your eyes to line up the crosses that appear and try to merge the colors.

Chimerial - Click on a preset, or enter a custom target color (also provide a contrast color for hyperbolics) and choose a type.
